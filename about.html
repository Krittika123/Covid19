How did Covid-19 originate?

Severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 (SARS-CoV-2) is a novel severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus. It was first isolated from three people with pneumonia connected to the cluster of acute respiratory illness cases in Wuhan which is in China. All structural features of the novel SARS-CoV-2 virus particle occur in related coronaviruses in nature.
